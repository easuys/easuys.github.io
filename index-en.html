EA Suys - Structures and building services

EA Suys is a building consultancy based in Kapelle-op-den-Bos, Belgium, with structural studies for residential and public building projects as its main activity, both renovation and new-build. We work quickly, carefully and at a fair price. The company is young, but its managing engineer has more than 15 years of experience in these projects. We also advise clients, architects and contractors on building services, energy, indoor climate, acoustics and installations.

Services

We support architects, clients and design teams with technical choices in renovation and new-build projects. The focus is on clear calculations, buildable details and realistic coordination between structure, building services and execution.

Structures and existing buildings

Structural studies for renovation and new construction, analysis of existing load-bearing structures, openings, timber floors, beams, foundations, terraces and temporary or permanent structural interventions.

Renovation and technical feasibility

Advice for alterations, reuse and renovation of existing buildings, with attention to load-bearing structure, execution, technical shafts, maintainability and phasing.

Building services: HVAC, sanitary and ventilation

Advice on heating and cooling concepts, ventilation, domestic hot water, pipework, technical shafts, PV, maintainability and correctly sized installations.

Energy and indoor climate

Support for heat loss, energy concepts, summer and winter comfort, moisture, condensation risks, thermal bridges, material choices and feasible renovation packages.

Acoustics

Indicative review of acoustic issues in renovation and new-build projects, including separating walls, floors, technical installations and comfort in use.

Tender review and site follow-up

Technical review of tenders, comparison of proposed solutions, coordination between structure and installations, design-team coordination and site follow-up.
